About Me

I'm Luz Angelique and I'm a Full-Stack Software Developer

I have had 3 fruitful years of experience as a pediatric registered nurse in inpatient and outpatient settings in the healthcare field prior to establishing my journey in software development. I cherished the indispensable experiences and opportunities that healthcare gave me to provide holistic and culturally competent care to others while making a beneficial impact in people's lives and health. It enabled me to be resilient and honed my critical thinking aptitude to solve complex problems by implementing logic while collaborating in a team setting. Having the fortuity of shadowing a software engineer who introduced me to software development made me realize that the tech industry would enable me to implement the skills that I have developed with my passion for life-long learning, creativity, and helping others. As a software engineer, nothing makes me more fulfilled than knowing I can make a positive difference by developing software applications with meaningful user experience that can help improve our quality of life as I continue to grow and learn new technologies.
